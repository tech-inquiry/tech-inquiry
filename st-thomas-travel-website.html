    • Weather

    When coming to the Virgin Islands you would realize that the temperatures are usually sunny and hot. Between December and February is the best time to go to St. Thomas. Temperatures are pleasant, averaging 79℉ (26℃). December can see some rain, mainly in short, sharp late afternoon showers as humidity builds up, but this decreases through January and February.
